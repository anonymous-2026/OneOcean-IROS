## How we inject our data (what makes it “ours”)

For H2 “official” task runs (see `tracks/h2_holoocean/run_plume_tasks.py`):

- **Currents**: sampled from our `combined_environment.nc` (default: tiny variant) and injected as a planar force bias on each AUV (proxy for drift/drag forcing).
- **Pollution field**: generated by our **OCPNet PollutionModel3D** advection–diffusion solver (repo copy under `OCPNet/PollutionModel3D/`), with the solver velocity field resampled from our dataset `uo/vo`.
  - We warm up the field so it is visible and provides non-degenerate probe signals.
  - For containment/cleanup we freeze the source after warmup (spill stops) and apply an agent-driven sink to approximate cleanup/skimmer effects.
  - Ground-truth target for localization/containment is the **post-warmup concentration hotspot** (argmax on the solver grid), which is more stable than “source position” under advection.

Implementation notes:
- World coordinates are taken directly from HoloOcean `PoseSensor`. The PollutionModel3D depth axis is treated as a **relative depth** coordinate (0 at `depth_min`), and we convert between “absolute depth” and solver depth internally.
